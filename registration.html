Payment Information

Accepted Payment Methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer (for group registrations)
  • Company Purchase Orders

Payment Schedule:

  • Individual registrations: Full payment due at registration
  • Group registrations (5+): 50% deposit, balance due 2 weeks before event
  • Corporate bookings: Net 30 payment terms available
Refund & Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Timeline:

  • More than 30 days before event: Full refund
  • 15-30 days before event: 75% refund
  • 7-14 days before event: 50% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund (credit for future events)

Transfer Policy:

Event transfers to another person are allowed up to 3 days before the event at no additional cost.
